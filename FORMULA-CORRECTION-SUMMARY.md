## Corrected Formula

### Official Thai AQHI Formula
```
AQHI = (10 / C) √ó Total %ER

Where:
  C = 105.19 (Thai scaling constant)
  Total %ER = %ER_PM2.5 + %ER_O3 + %ER_NO2
  %ER_i = 100 √ó (e^(Œ≤i √ó xi) - 1)
```

```javascript
  return Math.max(1, Math.round(aqhi));
```
